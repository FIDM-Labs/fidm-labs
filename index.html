Welcome

Welcome to the FIDM Labs website, supporting students and faculty in Fashion, Interior Design, and Merchandising at Central Michigan University. Explore our labs, services, tutorials, and student employment opportunities.

Labs

FIDM Labs include the Sewing Lab, Innovation & Tech Lab, Merchandising Lab, and Interior Design Studio. Each space supports different aspects of student creativity and hands-on learning.

Services

We provide 3D printing, poster printing, sewing machine access, garment construction tools, equipment checkout, and technical support for lab tools and software.

Tutorials

Access guides and walkthroughs for lab equipment, software setup, and common workflows. Additional tutorials will be linked here as they become available.

Employment

Interested in becoming a lab assistant? Learn more about responsibilities, expectations, and how to apply for student employment in our labs.
